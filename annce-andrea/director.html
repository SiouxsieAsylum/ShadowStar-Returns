James McKenzie

A recent graduate from the Macauley Honors Bachelors of Arts for Film program for CUNY Brooklyn College, James McKenzie is a director, producer, and visionary. He specialies in dynamic cinematography and character coaching with his actors. His focus on promoting diversity and subverting tropes has won Shadowstar Returns the Audience award at the Brooklyn College Film Festival and a coveted space at the New York Short Film Tuesdays Festival.
